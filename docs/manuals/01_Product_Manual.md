# Purpose

This manual serves as the primary business reference for the WAMbot platform.

It explains the product vision, business objectives, target users, feature strategy, competitive positioning, and long-term roadmap.

Every developer, designer, AI assistant, and stakeholder should read this document before working on the project.

---

# Product Summary

WAMbot is a commercial SaaS platform that enables businesses to automate customer communication using WhatsApp and AI.

The platform combines:

- AI-powered conversations
- WhatsApp automation
- Marketing campaigns
- Customer support
- Customer retention
- Customer segmentation
- Win-back campaigns
- Broadcast messaging
- Shopify integration
- WooCommerce integration

The goal is to become an all-in-one customer engagement platform for ecommerce businesses.

---

# Business Vision

Our vision is to build the easiest and most intelligent WhatsApp automation platform for ecommerce brands.

Unlike traditional chatbot platforms, WAMbot focuses on increasing customer lifetime value through AI-powered marketing automation rather than only answering customer questions.

# Core Business Objectives

The platform should help businesses:

- Increase customer retention
- Recover abandoned carts
- Win back inactive customers
- Automate customer support
- Increase repeat purchases
- Improve customer engagement
- Reduce support costs
- Generate higher revenue from existing customers

---

# Key Differentiators

WAMbot is designed around customer lifecycle marketing rather than simple chatbot automation.

Major differentiators include:

- AI-first architecture
- Native WhatsApp automation
- Customer segmentation
- Ecommerce integrations
- Campaign automation
- Broadcast messaging
- Customer analytics
- Future multi-channel expansion
